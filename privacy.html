Privacy Policy

How this website handles data, cookies, and advertising.

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Overview

This website (s-iguchi09.github.io) publishes technical articles and open-source software. It requires no registration and does not ask visitors to submit personal information. This policy explains the limited data that may be processed when you visit — primarily in connection with advertising.

Data This Site Collects

Except when you contact the developer by email (see the Contact section below), the site operator does not collect or store personal information through your use of the site, and the site does not run its own analytics or tracking. It is hosted on GitHub Pages; like any web host, GitHub may record standard server access logs (such as IP address, browser type, and access time) for security and operational purposes. See GitHub's Privacy Statement for details.

Cookies

This site sets no first-party cookies of its own for tracking. Any cookies stored in your browser during a visit originate from the third-party advertising described below. You can view, manage, or block cookies at any time through your browser settings; blocking cookies does not prevent you from reading the content.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies — and may also use web beacons, IP addresses, and other identifiers — to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You can control or opt out of personalized advertising through the following:

  • Google Ads Settings — manage the ads Google shows you.
  • aboutads.info / Your Online Choices (EU) — opt out of third-party ad personalization.

For more on how Google handles data when you use this site, see How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services and Google's Privacy & Terms. Visitors in the EEA, the UK, and Switzerland can manage ad personalization through the settings above.
